Free Agency Dashboard
This year's NBA free agency has been absolutely insane. This Summer's NBA Free Agency has made everyone wish that they where 6'10" and could shoot .400% from the three point line, becuase in today's NBA free agency market you would be making a minimum of 20 million dollars a years. (yes I said "A Year").
The NBA's Salary cap jumped 24 million dollars this offseason from 70 million dollars to 94 million dollars a 34.3% singular jump. The Salary cap jump gave owners an extra 720 million per year to spend. In total NBA owners have signed contracts worth 3.44 billion dollars in contracts.
This is a free agency dashboard that lets you easily visualize every single free agency transaction quickly.
On the left is a chord diagram that allows you to easily see the flow of NBA free agents, and their movement between teams. Chord widths at team widths are sized according to player/teams proportion of the whole whether a player's contract compared to all money spent this offseason or last years win share. Scroll over a specific team to discover that team's specific transaction flow.
On the Right is a chart where you can discover every single individual free agent signing this offseason. I made it searchable too. Scroll over the chart to discover exactly where a player has moved too and what his contract is.
